Manga Title: Lovers High: Shinyuu no Kareshi to Matching Shite Shimatta

Tags: Drama, Romance

Manga Description: Hikaru, a university senior grappling with job hunting and a lack of romance, delves into a dating app after her best friend finds love. Connecting with the attractive Kento through the app, they share a memorable night together. Hikaru's hopes for a budding romance shatter when she discovers Kento is, in fact, her friend's significant other.[anibrain.ai]


Anime Of The Day: Sagrada Reset

Anime Description: In the coastal city of Sakurada, Kei Asai resides amongst people gifted with peculiar powers from birth. At his school's rooftop, he encounters Misora Haruki, a disinterested girl capable of resetting time up to three days. While Haruki remains unaware of her resets, Kei, armed with a photographic memory, remembers each altered moment. Collaborating to assist others using their abilities, they join the Service Club. Soon, their altruistic endeavors intertwine with the enigmatic Administration Bureau, an entity manipulating Sakurada's gifted populace for undisclosed motives. Unveiling deeper mysteries beyond the Bureau's schemes, Kei and Haruki enter a realm where secrets lurk beneath the surface of their extraordinary town. [anibrain.ai]
