Error Codes
Error Codes and What they mean.

• 401 Unauthorized - The page you are trying to access is restricted to authorized personel only

• 404 Page Not Found - The page you are trying to visit doesn't exist.

• 502 Bad Gateway - Your request was received, but the internal server failed and the action wasn't completed

If you encounter an unlisted error please report it to the administators in our discord server, we will work to get it fixed and listed
