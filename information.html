About Ichiban | 一番

This website is still under development, please be patient. If you would like to help in the development you can do so in various ways, donations, advice, or joining the development team.

Email: Ichibanstudios.us@gmail.com

Ichiban is an anime streaming website created and developed by Ichiban Studios. We have daily anime recommendations, Anime Streaming, and an online merchandise store. Ichiban was created 9/11/2020. Ichiban is split into two seperate companies. The Website Development side is located in America, they are who make the website and upload the anime for you to watch. Anime by your favorite studios such as UFO Table, 8Bit Studios, Ghibli Studios and many more will be coming soon. Ichiban Studios, The Anime Production side, will be located in Japan because we also plan to produce our own anime.
